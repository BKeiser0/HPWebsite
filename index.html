Welcome to Harbor Point

Nestled along the pristine shores of Lake Michigan, Harbor Point Golf Club offers an unparalleled golfing experience in Harbor Springs, Michigan. Our championship 18-hole course combines breathtaking natural beauty with challenging play, creating memories that last a lifetime.

Established as one of Northern Michigan's premier golf destinations, we pride ourselves on exceptional course conditions, warm hospitality, and stunning panoramic views of Little Traverse Bay. Whether you're a scratch golfer or just beginning your journey, our course provides the perfect setting for every skill level.

Come discover why Harbor Point Golf Club has become the jewel of Harbor Springs golf courses, where every round is an adventure and every visit feels like coming home.

Club Amenities

Pro Shop

Fully stocked with the latest golf equipment, apparel, and accessories from top brands. Our PGA professionals are available for equipment fitting and advice.

Clubhouse Restaurant

Enjoy delicious dining with panoramic views of the course and bay. Perfect for post-round meals or special events.

Event Hosting

Beautiful facilities for weddings, corporate events, and tournaments. Our experienced staff will ensure your event is memorable.

Golf Instruction

Professional lessons available from our PGA certified instructors. Individual and group lessons for all skill levels.

Cart Rentals

Modern, well-maintained golf carts available for rent. GPS-enabled carts provide yardages and course information.

Member Programs

Various membership options available including seasonal, annual, and corporate packages with exclusive benefits.
